The Founder

Meet Matthew Gaudry

Gospel Detailing was built on a simple idea: do excellent work, treat people right, and let that be its own kind of good news. Headquartered in McDonough, GA, owner Matthew Gaudry started this business to bring premium, honest, mobile car care to Henry County and South Metro Atlanta — one driveway at a time.

Every detail is done with the same care whether it's a daily commuter or a weekend cruiser. No upsells you don't need, no shortcuts, no hassle — just a clean car and a good experience. That standard doesn't change based on the vehicle, the neighborhood, or the size of the job.
