FAQ

Full Detailing Questions

How long does a Full Detailing take?

Most Full Detailing appointments run 1.5–3 hours depending on the size of the vehicle and how much buildup we're working with.

Is Full Detailing worth it over a Basic Wash?

If it's been a while since your last real clean, or you want the wheels, windows, and interior handled in one visit, Full Detailing is the better value. Basic Wash is a lighter, faster refresh.
