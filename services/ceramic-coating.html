Ceramic Coating in McDonough, GA

Ceramic Coating is the long-term answer to keeping paint looking freshly detailed. It's a durable layer of protection applied on top of clean, corrected paint that beads water, resists UV fade, and makes future washes easier.

FAQ

Ceramic Coating Questions

Do I need Paint Correction before Ceramic Coating?

If your paint has visible swirls or scratches, we recommend Paint Correction first so the coating goes on top of a truly clean, smooth surface.

How long does ceramic coating last?

Longevity depends on the product used, how the vehicle is stored, and washing habits. We'll walk you through what to expect for your specific vehicle when we quote the job.
