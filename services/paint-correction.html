FAQ

Paint Correction Questions

Will Paint Correction remove all my scratches?

Light swirls and surface scratches typically correct very well. Deeper scratches that catch a fingernail may need touch-up paint in addition to correction — we'll be upfront about what to expect.

Should I get Ceramic Coating after Paint Correction?

It's a great combination. Correction restores the paint, and ceramic coating locks in that finish and protects it going forward.
