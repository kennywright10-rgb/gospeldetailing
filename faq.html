FAQ

Good Questions, Straight Answers

Everything you need to know about booking, pricing, and how mobile detailing works with Gospel Detailing.

Is Gospel Detailing based in McDonough, GA?

Yes. Gospel Detailing is headquartered at 300 Highway 81 W in McDonough, Georgia, in Henry County. McDonough is our home base and where we do the most work, though we also serve the wider South Metro Atlanta area.

Do you really come to my house?

Yes. Gospel Detailing is 100% mobile — we bring our equipment, water, and power to your driveway, office, or apartment complex anywhere in McDonough and the greater South Metro Atlanta service area.

What do I need to provide?

Nothing, in most cases — our unit carries its own water and power. If you'd prefer we use your outdoor spigot, that works too. Just make sure we have room to work around the vehicle.

How long does a detail take?

A Basic Wash typically runs 30-60 minutes. A Full Detailing service runs 1.5-3 hours depending on vehicle size and condition. Add-ons like carpet shampoo or paint correction extend the timeline.

What areas do you serve?

Our home base is McDonough, GA, and we serve all of Henry County plus the wider South Metro Atlanta area, including Stockbridge, Locust Grove, Hampton, Jonesboro, Fayetteville, Peachtree City, Conyers, Griffin, and Forest Park. Not sure if you're in range? Text us your zip code and we'll confirm.

What if it rains on my appointment day?

We'll reach out to reschedule at no charge if weather won't allow a quality result. Your convenience and your vehicle's finish come first.

How does the membership work?

Choose Weekly ($60), Bi-Weekly ($80), or Monthly ($100) and we'll keep your vehicle consistently clean on a set schedule. Cancel or pause anytime by texting us — no contracts.

Do I need Paint Correction before Ceramic Coating?

If your paint has visible swirls or scratches, we recommend Paint Correction first so the coating goes on top of a truly clean, smooth surface.

How do I pay?

We currently accept cash, credit, and debit. Ask us about payment options when you book.

How do I book an appointment?

The fastest way is to call or text (404) 594-9262. You can also message us on Instagram at @gospel_detailingllc or email info@gospeldetailing.com.
