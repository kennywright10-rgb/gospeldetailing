Pricing FAQ

Common Questions

Why is Full Detailing a price range instead of a flat rate?

Price depends on vehicle size and condition — a compact sedan and a 3-row SUV take different time and product. We confirm the exact price with a quick walk-around before starting, so there are no surprises.

Do you charge a travel fee?

Pricing above covers our standard South Metro Atlanta service area. If you're on the outer edge of our coverage map, just call or text and we'll confirm before booking.

How does the referral discount work?

Refer a friend who books with us and you'll receive a Full Detailing for $100 flat, regardless of your vehicle's usual quoted price.
